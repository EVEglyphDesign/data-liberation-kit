# Jurisdiction: Privacy Act / APPs (Australia)

**Statute:** Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) — Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
**Right:** Access to personal information — **APP 12**
**Regulator:** Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

## Response clock

- **30 days** for organizations (APP entities) to give access after the request (APP 12.4(b)).
- (Agencies must respond within 30 days under APP 12.4(a).)

## Scope

- Access to the personal information the entity holds about the individual.

## Cost

- Must not charge for *making* the request; a reasonable, non-excessive charge may apply for *giving* access (APP 12.8).

## Escalation path (maps to DSAR ladder)

- **R2 non-compliance:** 30-day deadline passes without adequate response or valid refusal.
- **R3 regulator:** complaint to the **OAIC**.

## Clock formula

`deadline = sent_date + 30 days`
