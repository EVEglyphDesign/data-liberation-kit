# Jurisdiction: LGPD (Brazil)

**Statute:** Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), Lei nº 13.709/2018
**Right:** Right of access / confirmation of processing — Art. 18 (I, II)
**Regulator:** Autoridade Nacional de Proteção de Dados (ANPD).

## Response clock

- **Simplified form:** immediately on request.
- **Complete declaration** (clear and complete, indicating origin, criteria, and purpose): within **15 days** of the request (Art. 19, II).

## Scope

- Confirmation that processing exists; access to the data; information on public/private entities the controller shared data with.

## Cost

- Free of charge.

## Escalation path (maps to DSAR ladder)

- **R2 non-compliance:** 15-day deadline passes without adequate response.
- **R3 regulator:** petition to the **ANPD**.

## Clock formula

`deadline = sent_date + 15 days`
