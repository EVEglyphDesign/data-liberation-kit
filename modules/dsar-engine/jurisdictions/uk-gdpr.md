# Jurisdiction: UK GDPR + Data Protection Act 2018 (United Kingdom)

**Statute:** UK GDPR + Data Protection Act 2018
**Right:** Right of access (DSAR) — UK GDPR Article 15
**Regulator:** Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk

## Response clock

- **1 calendar month** from the day the request is received.
- Extension: **up to 2 further months** for complex or numerous requests; must inform the individual within the first month.
- If the organization needs information to confirm identity, the clock can pause until that is provided.
- Source: [ICO — Time limits for responding to data protection rights requests](https://ico.org.uk/for-the-public/time-limits-for-responding-to-data-protection-rights-requests/)

## Cost

- **Free** in most cases; a "reasonable fee" only for manifestly unfounded/excessive or repeat requests.

## Escalation path (maps to DSAR ladder)

- **R2 non-compliance:** 1-month (or extended 3-month) deadline passes.
- **R3 regulator:** complain to the **ICO**.

## Clock formula

`deadline = sent_date + 1 calendar month` · `extended_deadline = sent_date + 3 calendar months (with notice in first month)`
